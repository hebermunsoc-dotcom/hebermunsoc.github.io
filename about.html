About Heber MUN Society

A platform rooted in diplomacy, dialogue, and disciplined leadership.

Our Vision

The vision of Heber Model United Nations Society (Heber MUNSOC) is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves a voice. Our mantra, "All Means All, Leave No One Behind," reflects our unwavering commitment to inclusivity and equal opportunity. We strive to ensure that every student, regardless of background, can participate and thrive in the domain of international diplomacy. Our goal is to empower students to become informed, compassionate global citizens who collaborate to address pressing global challenges. By fostering diversity and cooperation, we prepare our members to lead with integrity and effectuate a positive impact on the world.

Our Mission

Heber Model United Nations Society (Heber MUNSOC) is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders by providing a structured platform for students to engage in global diplomacy, enhance critical thinking, and develop a profound understanding of international affairs. The Society endeavours to create an inclusive and dynamic environment that upholds professionalism, discipline, and excellence in communication, thereby enabling members to contribute meaningfully to the world as informed and responsible global citizens.

Our Origin

Established within Bishop Heber College, Heber MUN Society emerged from the collective aspiration to institutionalize diplomatic simulations as a serious academic exercise in 2022. The Society was founded to foster disciplined deliberation, research-driven debate, and leadership development among students.

Secretariat - 2025-26

The Secretariat serves as the executive board of the Society.

Comprising experienced delegates, chairs, and organizers, the Secretariat is responsible for academic integrity, conference management, and institutional continuity. Each member is committed to upholding the core values of excellence, accountability, and academic rigor.

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike conventional student clubs, Heber MUN Society emphasizes research depth, procedural accuracy, and institutional professionalism. Our conferences and workshops are designed to mirror real-world diplomacy, ensuring participants gain practical and intellectual value.
